📗 What is a Stock?

A stock is a small piece of a company. If the company does well, your piece becomes worth more!

📈 Trend is your friend

Look at the chart. If the bars are climbing, the stock is in an uptrend. Buying into a healthy uptrend is safer than guessing.

📰 News moves prices

Watch the news bar! Good news (a new product, strong earnings) pushes a price up. Bad news pushes it down. React before everyone else.

💰 Buy Low, Sell High

Buy when a stock is cheap, sell when it's worth more. The difference is your profit.

Buy at $50 → Sell at $75 = $25 profit each share! 💰

🧺 Don't put all your eggs in one basket

Owning a few different stocks (diversifying) means one bad day won't wipe you out.
